2000
Sai Enterprises Founded
Operations began in Hyderabad with a focus on practical, production-ready graphic machinery. HPM partnership established from the outset — making Sai India's exclusive HPM agent. Founded by Mr. S. Dayaker Reddy and Mr. G. Phani Kumar.
2010
India Presence Expanded
Regional support expanded across New Delhi, Pune, and Vijayawada. Sales partners established across Kolkata, Mumbai, Ahmedabad, Jaipur, Bangalore, Coimbatore and Goa — shortening response time nationally.
2015
Kenya Office Established
Nairobi office strengthened East Africa support. Exports to Sri Lanka, Nepal, UAE, Oman and African countries cemented pan-world reach — a global supplier built from Hyderabad.
